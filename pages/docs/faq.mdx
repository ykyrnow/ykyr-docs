# FAQ

<details>
  <summary>What is YKYR?</summary>

  YKYR is a platform that empowers users to collect, own, and monetize their personal browsing data and behavioral traits.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How does YKYR protect my privacy?</summary>

  All data is encrypted locally on your device before being stored on IPFS. Only you have access to your decryption keys.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Is my data secure?</summary>

  Yes, YKYR uses client-side encryption and secure storage on IPFS. No unencrypted data is ever accessed or stored by YKYR.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>What kind of data does YKYR extension collect?</summary>

  YKYR extension collects basic browsing data such as:
  - URLs of websites you visit
  - Time spent on each website
  
  The extension does NOT collect:
  - Passwords or login credentials
  - Personal messages or emails
  - Financial information
  - Form data or keystrokes
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How do I get started?</summary>

  1. Download the [YKYR extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/ykyr/gfogbpcdlcihfaejpmdhigejhpckdamh)
  2. Create an account
  3. Turn on data collection
  4. Start contributing data and earn rewards
</details>
